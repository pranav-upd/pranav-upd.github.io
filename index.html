About Me

Hi I'm Pranav. An AI geek and I'm passionate in sharing my knowledge. My channel "Pranav Instructs" is a channel dedicated to simple, practical learning for curious minds. Here you will find clear explanations of technology, artificial intelligence and DSA (Data structures and algorithms) problem solving ideas that help you think better and build useful skills. The goal of this channel is to break down complex concepts into easy lessons that anyone can understand and apply. Whether you are a student, a self learner, or someone exploring new ideas, Pranav Instructs focuses on teaching step by step concepts, practical insights, and thoughtful discussions that help you grow intellectually and professionally. Learning here is simple, direct, and focused on real understanding rather than unnecessary complexity.
